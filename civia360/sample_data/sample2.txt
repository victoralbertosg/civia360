PROPERTY TAX, CIVIL REGISTRY AND BUSINESS REGISTRATION - MUNICIPAL GUIDE
=========================================================================

SECTION 1 - PROPERTY TAX (IMPUESTO PREDIAL)

Property tax is the annual contribution that all owners of real estate properties
(houses, land, commercial premises) located in the municipality must pay.

PAYMENT DEADLINES
- January: 15% discount on the annual total (early payment).
- February: 10% discount.
- March through June: payment without discount and without surcharge.
- July onwards: 2% monthly surcharge applied cumulatively.

HOW TO PAY PROPERTY TAX
Payment can be made through the following channels:
  1. In person: Municipal Treasury, Av. Independencia No. 55,
     Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM.
  2. Online: Payment portal at www.municipio.gob.mx/predial
     with credit or debit card (no extra charge).
  3. Banks and convenience stores: using the payment reference issued by the Treasury.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR PAYMENT
  - Cadastral account number (found on previous receipts or property deeds).
  - Official identification of the owner.

EXEMPTIONS AND SPECIAL DISCOUNTS
  - Persons over 60 years old: 50% discount with valid INAPAM credential.
  - Persons with disabilities: 50% discount with official medical certificate.
  - Properties for social use (public schools, hospitals): exempt from payment.

SECTION 2 - CIVIL REGISTRY: BIRTH CERTIFICATE

The birth certificate is the official document that certifies the birth of a person
and establishes their legal identity.

HOW TO REQUEST A BIRTH CERTIFICATE
  Visit the Civil Registry located at the Municipal Palace, Upper Floor,
  with the following documents:
    1. Official identification of the applicant.
    2. CURP (unique population registry code) of the registered person, if available.
    3. Proof of payment of fees ($50 pesos per certified copy).

The process is immediate when the birth was registered in this municipality.
For births registered in other municipalities, a formal request must be submitted
to the corresponding Civil Registry (estimated time: 10 business days).

SECTION 3 - BUSINESS REGISTRATION

To register a business in the municipality the following documents must be submitted:
  1. Business opening application form (available free at the DDU office).
  2. Official identification of the owner or legal representative.
  3. Articles of incorporation for legal entities (companies).
  4. Proof of address of the business (electricity, water, or phone bill).
  5. Land use certificate showing compatibility with the commercial activity.
  6. Favorable civil protection assessment.
  7. Structural safety certificate for premises larger than 100 sqm.

Processing time is 10 business days.
The permit is valid for 1 year and must be renewed annually.

SECTION 4 - LAND USE REGULATIONS

RESIDENTIAL USE
In areas zoned as H1 (single-family residential) the following are allowed:
  - Single-family housing.
  - Mixed-use offices on the ground floor (maximum 30% of the built area).
  - Small neighborhood shops without storage of hazardous materials.

In H2 zones (multi-family residential):
  - Apartment buildings of up to 4 floors.
  - Low-impact educational and health services.

COMMERCIAL USE
C1 zones (neighborhood commerce) allow:
  - Grocery stores, stationery shops, pharmacies.
  - Artisan workshops without noise or hazardous waste emissions.

C2 zones (corridor commerce) allow:
  - Supermarkets, department stores.
  - Restaurants and entertainment services.
  - Public parking lots.

INDUSTRIAL USE
I1 zones (light industry) allow low environmental impact manufacturing.
I2 zones (heavy industry) require an Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
approved by the State Ministry of Environment.

GENERAL RESTRICTIONS IN RESIDENTIAL AREAS
  - Warehouses, auto repair shops, and gas depots are not allowed.
  - Buildings must not exceed the height established in the Urban Development Plan (UDP):
    maximum 3 floors in H1 and 5 floors in H2 zones.
  - Minimum open area in residential lots is 20% of the total land area.
  - Parking must comply with 1 space per every 80 sqm of built area.
